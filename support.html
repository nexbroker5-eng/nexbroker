Getting Started
NexTrade is a modern online trading platform designed for both beginners and experienced traders. Whether you are taking your first steps in financial markets or managing an advanced portfolio, NexTrade provides the tools, instruments, and support to help you trade with confidence.
Creating an account is straightforward. Click the "Get Started" button, complete the registration form with your personal details, verify your identity, and fund your account. The entire process takes just a few minutes.
NexTrade is available to traders in most countries worldwide. During registration, you will be informed if there are any restrictions applicable to your region.
Trading
NexTrade offers a wide range of trading instruments including Forex currency pairs, commodities such as Gold, Silver, and Crude Oil, and major cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and more.
NexTrade offers some of the most competitive spreads in the market. Our spreads start from as low as 0.0 pips on major currency pairs, ensuring you keep more of your profits on every trade.
Copy Trading allows you to automatically replicate the trades of experienced and verified traders on the platform. Simply browse our list of top-performing traders, select the one whose strategy suits your goals, and allocate funds to copy their trades in real time. It is an ideal feature for beginners who want to benefit from expert knowledge while still learning the markets.
NexTrade offers flexible leverage options depending on the instrument and your account type. Leverage details are clearly displayed when placing a trade. Please note that while leverage can increase potential profits, it also increases risk.
Deposits & Withdrawals
Log in to your account and navigate to the Deposit section. Select your preferred payment method, enter the amount, and follow the on-screen instructions. Deposits are typically processed instantly or within a few hours depending on the method used.
NexTrade supports multiple payment methods including bank transfers, credit and debit cards, and cryptocurrency transfers. Available methods will be displayed in the Deposit section of your account.
Withdrawal requests are reviewed and processed within 24 to 48 business hours. The time it takes for funds to arrive in your account may vary depending on your chosen withdrawal method and your financial institution.
Yes. Minimum amounts apply to both deposits and withdrawals depending on your account type and chosen payment method. These details are clearly outlined during the transaction process.
Security & Verification
Yes. NexTrade employs industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect all personal and financial data. Your information is never shared with unauthorised third parties.
Identity verification is a regulatory requirement designed to protect you and the integrity of the platform. It helps prevent fraud, money laundering, and unauthorised account access. Verification is quick and requires a government-issued ID and proof of address.
KYC verification is typically completed within 24 hours of submitting the required documents. You will be notified by the platform once your verification is approved.
Account & Support
NexTrade offers different account tiers suited to different levels of experience and trading capital. You can upgrade your account type as your trading needs grow.
On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your registered email address. You will receive a password reset link within minutes.
Our support team is available through the live chat feature on the platform, as well as through the Submit a Request form below. We are committed to responding to all enquiries promptly.
NexTrade does not charge hidden fees. Any applicable fees are transparently displayed before you confirm any transaction or account action.
